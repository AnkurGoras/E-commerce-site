Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting the "My Orders" section in your account dashboard.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, debit cards, credit cards, net banking, and selected wallets.

How long does a refund take?

Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days after product verification.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled before they are shipped from our warehouse.

What is your return policy?

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they meet return conditions.

Is my payment information secure?

All transactions are secured using encrypted payment gateways.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Cash on Delivery is available for selected products and locations.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us via the Contact Us page, email, or phone support.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship within India and selected international regions.

What if I receive a damaged product?

Please raise a return request within 48 hours with images.

Can I update my delivery address?

Address changes are allowed before order dispatch.

How do I apply coupons?

Coupons can be applied at checkout before payment.

Will I receive an invoice?

A digital invoice is generated after order confirmation.

Are there hidden charges?

No, all charges are transparently shown during checkout.

How can I update my account details?

You can update details in the Profile section of your account.
